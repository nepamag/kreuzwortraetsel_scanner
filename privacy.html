Privacy Policy — Kreuzworträtsel Scanner

Last updated: 2026-05-17

Summary

Kreuzworträtsel Scanner runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, photos, or scan results.

Data Collection

This app collects no personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising identifiers, and no crash reporting are used. The app does not communicate with any server.

Camera and Storage Access

The app requests access to your camera and image storage only when you explicitly choose to take a photo or select an image to scan. The selected image is processed locally on your device using on-device optical character recognition. Images are not uploaded anywhere and are not retained beyond your current session.

Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
